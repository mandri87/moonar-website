Taire Designer Suite

Complete Solution for Car & Truck Tire Design

Taire Designer Suite is a comprehensive design platform specifically developed for passenger car and truck tire engineering. The suite integrates multiple specialized tools that work seamlessly together to streamline the entire design workflow, from initial concept to final production specifications.

  • Integrated Workflow: Complete toolchain covering mould design, section design, and die design for both car and truck applications.
  • AI-Powered Intelligence: Leverages artificial intelligence to bridge the gap between 2D flat components and 3D vulcanized geometries.
  • Automated Precision: Reduces manual work and human error through intelligent automation while maintaining full designer control.

The suite represents a new generation of tire design tools that combine traditional engineering expertise with cutting-edge AI technology, enabling designers to work faster and smarter.

Mould Designer Plus

Revolutionizing Tire Mould Design

Mould Designer Plus is an advanced tool that revolutionizes the tire mould design process, offering unparalleled speed, accuracy, and scalability. Starting from the alignment definition, the tool automates the entire design workflow, delivering fully dimensioned DXF files, estimations of inflated dimensions, and precise tire weight calculations.

  • Rapid Mould Creation: Generate a complete mould design in just a few seconds, significantly reducing time-to-market.
  • Automated Validation: Instantly verify dimensional accuracy and tire weight, ensuring optimal performance.
  • Product Line Extension: Develop a full product line by defining only a few key sizes. Extend the alignment to all sizes in the line within minutes, maintaining consistency and precision.
  • Car & Truck Support: Full compatibility with both passenger car and truck tire specifications.

Mould Designer Plus empowers tire designers to streamline their workflow, achieve exceptional efficiency, and focus on innovation. Whether you're working on a single tire or an entire product line, this tool delivers results that are fast, reliable, and tailored to your needs.

Section Designer Plus

Precision Section Design for Car & Truck

Section Designer Plus is a specialized tool for designing tire cross-sections with integrated die design capabilities. Available for both passenger car and truck applications, it provides complete control over the internal architecture of the tire, from rubber compounds to reinforcement materials.

  • Integrated Die Designer: Includes built-in die (bocchettone) design capabilities for creating precise tread pattern outlets.
  • Car & Truck Versions: Optimized workflows for both passenger car and commercial truck tire designs.
  • Component Precision: Design rubber compounds, textiles, and steel components with exact geometric control.
  • Vulcanized Geometry: Represents components as they appear in the vulcanized state, pushed against the curved mould surface.

Section Designer Plus provides the detailed control needed for complex tire architectures while maintaining the speed and efficiency required in modern tire development.
